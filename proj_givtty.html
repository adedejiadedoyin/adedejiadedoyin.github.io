Givtty

Get gifts right every time

RoleFullStack Developer
TypeMobile Hybrid (Web) App
PeriodNov,2019

Project

Givtty is a mobile App that helps users create a ‘what-to-get-me’ gift-list which can be shared with friends and families. It helps users (gift givers) know exactly what to give to their loved ones thereby eliminating guesses and the amount of time spent in brainstorming a “special” gift. On the other hand Givtty allows users to create a Wishlist that can be viewed by friends, families and acquaintances.
