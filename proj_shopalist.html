ShopaList.

A minimal but effective Shopper's companion

Shopalist is a mobile app that makes it very easy to create multiple shopping lists. You can create a list for each store that you shop at. Use it for grocery shopping or any other shopping needs that you may have.

It is easy to use and improves on the cons (weaknesses) of other shopping list apps.

Other notable benefits of Shopalist include:

  • Works offline without internet.
  • Item suggestions for quick addition of items.
  • Arrange items in order by drag and drop.
  • Easily Navigate through your shopping lists.

Technologies used

  • Ionic 4
  • Angular8 & Typescript
  • Capacitor
  • SQLite Database
  • Git(GitHub)
