Shopalist

Simple shopping list mobile app

Shopalist is a mobile app that makes it very easy to create multiple shopping lists. You can create a list for each store that you shop at. Use it for grocery shopping or any other shopping needs that you may have. It is easy to use and improves on the cons (weaknesses) of other shopping list apps.
